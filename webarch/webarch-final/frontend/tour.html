Stress Free Colors

Color affects the way we feel. It can affect our moods in either a positive or negative manner. After a great research about color stress reduction , our team came up with a perfect color pallet . With the use of Colored overlays we found that the visual stress is reduced and speed of reading is increased. And the more use of calm colors and less use of bright luminous colors we were able to give our users the best experience of stress free colors.
